INTRODUCTION                            *take-me-to-your-leader*

Leader commands are an incredibly valuable tool, and as such users will generate
many of them. This plugin will help keep them organized and tidy.

To use this plugin, users first have to do 3 things:
1. Wrap their list of leader commands in a preceding comment that includes
"leader-begin", and follow the comment with an empty line, and a succeeding
comment that includes "leader-end" and precede the comment with an empty line.
